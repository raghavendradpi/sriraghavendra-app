About Temple

Sri Raghavendra Swamy Temple is located in Bharathipuram Dharmapuri,constructed on January 1st 1995, we are celebrating Aradhana for past {{ sinceConstruction }} years.

The Sacred Mrittika Brindavan was installed by H.H.Sri Sushmindra Tirtha Swamigal then Head of Sri Rayar Mutt, Manthralayam and H.H. Sri Vijnana Nidhi tirtha Swamigal then Head of Mulubagal Sri SriPadaraja Swamigal Mutt.

Every year during sri guru raghavedra swamy aradhana festicval we do annadhana to deveotess about 400 to 800 every day. During covid times as well we conducted annadhana for 2 months for many homeless people and sanitary workers.
